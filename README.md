Food-Delivery-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)

Project Objective
The Food Delivery project aims to create an annual orders report for 2024. So that, the owner of the Food Delivery business can understand their customers and grow more orders in 2025 & 2026.
Dataset used
-<a href="https://github.com/piyushprajapati01/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Food_Delivery_Dashboard_Data.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
-	Compare the orders across all 4 regions using a single chart.
-	Which region got the highest number of orders?
-	Which restaurant is contributing the maximum orders?
-	What is the order share percentage of each restaurant?
-	Which food item is ordered the most?
-	Relation between region and food item based on number of orders.
-	Which channel / region contributes the least to total orders?
-	Percentage of total orders delivered successfully.
-	Dashboard Interaction — View Dashboard (attach screenshot link)

Process
-	Verify data for any missing values and anomalies, and sort out the same.
-	Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data type, data format and values used.
-	Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.
-	Merge all pivot tables into one dashboard and apply slicer to make dynamic.

##Project Insight
-	South region leads with the highest order share at 28.49% of total orders.
-	West region follows at 25.15%, East at 22.93%, and North at 23.43%.
-	HotServe is the top performing restaurant with 7,291 total orders.
-	TastyBites and FreshPlate are strong competitors with 6,787 and 6,729 orders respectively.
-	MasalaBox has the lowest order count at 5,634 — indicating room for growth.
-	Popular food items include Biryani, Burger, Pizza, Momos, and Noodles across all regions.


##Final Conclusion:
To improve the overall order volume of the Food Delivery business, a focused marketing strategy should target the South and West regions which already show high demand. Restaurants like MasalaBox and YummyStop should introduce combo offers and discounts to increase their order share. The top-selling items — Biryani, Burger, and Pizza — should be promoted heavily on Swiggy, Zomato, and Amazon channels. Expanding the menu variety in the North region, which currently has the lowest share, can help balance regional performance and drive overall business growth.
